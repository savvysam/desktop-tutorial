About Sam

Technical foundation, editorial eye, and inclusive service

I’m Sam Strock, founder of Curl Up & Dye. My work centers on curl integrity, natural-looking blonding, and cuts that wear beautifully between appointments.

After advanced education in curl methodology and corrective color, I built an independent practice to give each guest focused one-on-one time. No rushed overlap, no generic formulas.

I welcome all identities, textures, and style expressions. The goal is always the same: hair that reflects you and supports your routine.

  • Advanced curl cutting education
  • Dimensional blonding and tone strategy specialist
  • Consultative appointments with custom maintenance plans
