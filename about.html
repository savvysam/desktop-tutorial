About Sam

Technical precision with a creative pulse.

I built Curl Up & Dye as an independent brand to give clients what’s often missing in bigger salons: focused attention, transparent communication, and hair design that matches real life.

My specialty work centers on curly texture, dimensional blonding, and modern shape architecture. I regularly pursue advanced education in curl science, lightening safety, and contemporary cutting techniques.

I welcome all genders, hair types, and identities. Every appointment is collaborative, respectful, and customized to your goals.

Approach

Consult first. Execute second. Refine always.

Education

Ongoing training in curl methodology, blonding chemistry, and editorial-inspired finishing.

Studio Vibe

Calm, inclusive, and creative with subtle Castro energy and a boutique feel.
