FAQ & Policies

Simple policies for a smooth appointment.

How do I book?

Use the booking link on the Contact page. If you are new and planning a major change, please start with a consultation.

What is the cancellation policy?

Please provide at least 48 hours’ notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations may be charged 50% of the reserved service.

What happens with no-shows?

No-shows may be charged 100% of the booked service and require prepayment for future appointments.

How should I arrive for my appointment?

Arrive on time with detangled hair. For curly cuts, come with hair clean, dry, and styled in your natural pattern unless instructed otherwise.

Where are you located and what about parking?

The studio is in San Francisco’s Castro district. Street parking is limited; MUNI is often the easiest option. Exact arrival details are shared in your confirmation email.
