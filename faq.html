FAQ & Policies

Clear expectations for a smooth appointment

How do I book an appointment?

Use the Book Now link to request your service. New color guests should select a consultation first.

What is the cancellation policy?

Changes within 48 hours are charged 50% of scheduled services. No-shows are charged 100%.

What should new clients expect?

Your first visit includes intake review, hair history, and a tailored plan for your texture and goals.

Do you offer consultations?

Yes. Consultations are encouraged for corrections, vivid shades, and major blonding projects.

Where are you located and how do I get there?

Curl Up & Dye is in San Francisco’s Castro area with easy Muni access and nearby paid parking garages.
