# SEO & Local Metadata Suggestions

## Page Titles
- Home: Curl Up & Dye | Curly Hair & Blonding Specialist in San Francisco
- Services: Services | Curl Up & Dye San Francisco
- Portfolio: Portfolio | Curl Up & Dye
- About: About Sam Strock | Curl Up & Dye
- Reviews: Reviews | Curl Up & Dye
- FAQ: FAQ & Policies | Curl Up & Dye
- Contact: Contact & Booking | Curl Up & Dye

## Meta Description Pattern
Use: service + location + differentiator + CTA.

Example:
"Curl Up & Dye by Sam Strock offers personalized curly cuts, blonding, and modern color services in San Francisco. Book your appointment today."

## Local SEO Essentials
- Keep consistent name: Curl Up & Dye
- Keep owner reference: Sam Strock
- Include city in key headings and body copy naturally
- Maintain visible NAP details on contact page
- Add alt text focused on style + service outcome
- Keep image file names descriptive where possible
